General Information

I moved to Calgary, Alberta, Canada, in 2002. In my professional life, I am an AE, while my passion and leisure time is spent painting abstract works of immense colour combinations, intense emotional and spiritual expression, and great clarity and perspective.

I think that each and every one of my paintings makes their own sounds, and tell a story that may be different than the one I intended, but can be as personal and meaningful to you, no matter what your eyes may see in this abstract piece.
